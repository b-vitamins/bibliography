# Source Quality Policy

## Preferred source order

1. Official proceedings page + official PDF
2. Publisher landing page + publisher PDF
3. arXiv abs + arXiv PDF (when no official venue PDF is available)

## Disallowed patterns unless no alternative exists

- Generic aggregator pages without clear provenance
- Links that do not identify the exact cited work
- Placeholder or speculative entries without verifiable publication records

## Minimum metadata confidence

- `title` and `author` must match the source exactly
- `year` must match publication venue/year
- For `inproceedings`, include `booktitle`
